Turnover impacts more than just hiring—it creates ripple effects across your entire program. Based on your inputs, here's how those costs are distributed:

Hiring & Training Costs

$—/ year est.

Staffing gaps can limit classroom capacity or disrupt continuity, affecting enrollment stability and tuition revenue. When families experience inconsistency, some may disenroll—reducing the tuition revenue your center depends on.
